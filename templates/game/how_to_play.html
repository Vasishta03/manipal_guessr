How to Play

A quick guide to ManipalGeoGuessr.

01

Enter your nickname

No account needed. Just type a nickname and pick how many rounds you want to play (3, 5, or 7).

02

Study the photo

A photo of a real location somewhere on or around the Manipal campus will be shown. Look for clues — buildings, signs, landmarks, surroundings.

03

Drop your pin

Click anywhere on the Manipal map to place your pin. You can drag it to fine-tune, or click Clear to try again.

04

Confirm your guess

Once you're happy with your pin, click Confirm Guess. Your score for that round is calculated based on how far your pin is from the real location.

05

Scoring

Each round is scored out of 5000 points. You get full marks for a perfect guess, and 0 points if you're more than 1 km away. The score decreases linearly in between.

4000+ pts → Excellent 2000–4000 pts → Good 0–2000 pts → Keep practising
